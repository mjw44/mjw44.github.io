Metallurgical Failure Analysis & Fracture Mechanics

I am currently finalizing two comprehensive forensic reports investigating structural component degradation. This work centers on Linear Elastic Fracture Mechanics (LEFM) and fractography to identify root causes of mechanical instability.

The analysis utilizes high-fidelity characterization techniques, including Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM) for micro-topography and Energy Dispersive X-Ray Spectroscopy (EDS) for elemental mapping and impurity detection.
