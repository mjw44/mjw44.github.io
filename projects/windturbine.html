A collaborative engineering effort in high-efficiency power generation.

Something Big is Coming.

Currently in the R&D phase, this project focuses on optimizing blade geometry and structural integrity for small-scale wind energy systems.

Lead Collaborators: Julian Whiteley & Emilio [Last Name]

Project Scope & Objectives

In tandem with Emilio, I am developing a high-performance wind turbine prototype designed for maximum power coefficient ($C_p$) at low tip-speed ratios. Our collaborative workflow splits the project into two critical engineering domains:

  • Aerodynamic Optimization: Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) analysis of airfoil profiles and pitch angles.
  • Structural Analysis: Materials testing and failure mode analysis of composite blade structures under high torque loads.
