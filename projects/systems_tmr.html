Thermal Regulation & Transient Analysis

Operating high-performance GPUs and power systems within a sealed aluminum chassis creates a significant thermal challenge. I have integrated a network of NTC thermistors to monitor internal air and component temperatures, providing the data necessary to validate our theoretical models.

$$ \rho V C_p \frac{dT}{dt} = \dot{Q}_{gen} - h A (T_{int} - T_{ext}) $$

Current Work: Heat Transfer Modeling

I am currently conducting a transient thermal analysis to identify the system's "time to critical temperature." I am evaluating whether the convective coefficient of the water-cooled chassis walls allows the system to reach a steady-state equilibrium before thermal throttling occurs.

This phase involves correlating live thermistor data with our energy balance equations to refine the thermal resistance values of the internal air volume.
