Technical Deep-Dive: Dynamics Failure Modes

In addition to my management responsibilities, I am leading the Failure Mode and Effects Analysis (FMEA) for the Dynamics sub-team. This involves identifying critical failure points in the suspension and steering linkages under extreme loading conditions.

By applying Fatigue Analysis and Finite Element Analysis (FEA), I am characterizing how stress concentrations in mounting parts react to high-impact landings—ensuring that the vehicle maintains integrity through the full 4-hour endurance event.
