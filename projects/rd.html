Role & Responsibilities

Cross-Team Coordination

As R&D Lead, I synchronized the technical roadmaps of five sub-teams: Powertrain, Dynamics, Electronics, Ergonomics, and Chassis. The core responsibility was preventing siloed design decisions by ensuring each team's work was documented, reviewed against vehicle-level requirements, and traceable to a shared performance objective.

Research Governance

Each sub-team was required to produce literature reviews, design justifications, and FMEA documentation before design freeze. I reviewed these documents for technical rigor, identified gaps in load assumptions or material selection logic, and coordinated cross-team reviews when a decision in one system affected another.
