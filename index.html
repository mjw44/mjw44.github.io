About

Hi! My name is Julian, and I'm a Mechanical Engineering Honors student at UT Austin. I've been focusing on thermal fluid systems, structural mechanics, and integrated system design. My main projects combine first principles and engineering analysis from Heat Transfer, Materials, Fluid Mechanics, and Mechanics of Solids. The application of these first principles spans structural buckling, fatigue analysis, convective heat transfer optimization, and closed-loop control systems. My internship at CEM, leadership role in Longhorn Baja Racing, and active membership in Texas Marine Robotics have given me exposure to end-to-end mechanical design projects with real constraints, budgets, and consequences. I am particularly interested in problems where fluid mechanics, heat transfer, and structural mechanics intersect with practical product implementation.
