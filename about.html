About

Engineering systems that push boundaries.

Hi, I’m Julian. I’m a Mechanical Engineering Honors student at The University of Texas at Austin. I thrive in the space where advanced simulation meets physical manufacturing, specializing in thermal-fluids, structural analysis, and design for manufacturing.

My engineering philosophy is rooted in validation and practical execution. I focus on building reliable systems that perform flawlessly under pressure.

Mechanical Engineering · UT Austin

Design

SolidWorks, DFM, tolerance thinking, rapid prototyping, BOM ownership, and design trade studies.

Current Work

At Integra, I am engineering a closed-loop fluid flow testing system and developing hydrostatic pressure testing protocols to validate structural integrity under operational loads. I also wrote a SolidWorks VBA macro that removed more than 100 hours of manual engineering overhead across 2,000+ legacy files.

In parallel, I lead simulation and validation work for Longhorn Baja Racing, where my team connects first-principles analysis, FEA, strain gauge data, and physical testing into one design workflow.
